How can I make a home server?

Do you have a decent amount of technical knowledge?
Do you have a spare family computer lying around that no one uses?
If yes, then you can make your own home server!

When I made my server I documented my entire process from start to finish as well as writing down all the youtube videos and websites I used. That can be downloaded here:
You will need a basic amount of computer knowledge, but that can be picked up through some of the youtube videos I provided. You should have a look at the 'Read Me first' Document and then the 'Quick Links' Doc after that.
